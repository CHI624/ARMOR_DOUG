Welcome to ETI

Your gateway to advanced risk management and analysis.

ETI Overview:

o Framework for artificial reasoning research and experimentation

o Provides access to reasoning modules for demonstrating basic research projects

o Provides access to reasoning modules for data collection and experimentation as use case prototypes

ETI

Current Modules:

o Activity Reasoning Module – demonstrating identification of uncertainty of information across multiple time series data

o Causal Multimodal Data (CMMD) – causally based classification of imagery and text data for supporting natural disaster tasks

o Adaptive Reasoning for Mitigating Operation Risks (ARMOR) – leverages large language models and causal graphs for supporting risk management

o Clarifying Causal Core Significance (C^3ORES) – enhanced causal object detection

Future Modules:

o Constrained Causal Decision Dilemma (C2D2)

o Research on Causal Casualty Outcomes and Vulnerabilities using Reasoning (REC 2 OVR)

o Dynamic Knowledge Augmented Graph Grounded Entity Reasoning (DAGGER)
